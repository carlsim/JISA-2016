About JISA

The Japanese International Students' Association (JISA) is a club at York University for anyone who is interested in Japan, its culture and society and/or its language to come together and live, laugh and learn. We strive to encourage cross-cultural communication between those in the York community and those in the greater Japanese community. We are a community where people with these interests can meet other like-minded people and hang out, making lifelong friends through the whole experience.

Why Join?

There are several reasons to join JISA.

Language Help

JISA offers help and tutoring for anyone wanting to learn Japanese

Events

JISA occasionally holds events throughout the school year

Community

Meet and make friends with others that have similar interests to you
